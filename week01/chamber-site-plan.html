About Our Chamber

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is:

  • To provide information about the Chamber of Commerce services.
  • To encourage membership and participation in business events.

User Scenarios

Key questions site visitors might have:

  • How can I join the Chamber of Commerce?
  • What upcoming business events are scheduled?
  • What are the benefits of being a Chamber member?

Color Scheme

Colors used:

  • Dark Blue (#002a5c) for the header and footer.
  • Gold (#ffcc00) to highlight the join button.

Typography

The 'Poppins' font is used for general site content, while 'Roboto' is applied to headings for a modern and professional style.

Design Description

This webpage design features a clear and organized structure to facilitate user navigation. Below are its main characteristics:

Header:

The header includes a dropdown menu with key options, a central logo, and a prominent "Join" button. Additionally, an anniversary message highlighting years of service is displayed.

Main Body:

  • An image carousel provides visual information about services or events.
  • A section of links arranged as a directory with images and descriptions highlights various areas of interest.
  • Explanatory text describes the use of CSS and the presentation of visual elements.

Footer:

Includes copyright information and the date of the last update. The responsive design ensures proper viewing on mobile devices.

The design combines a corporate blue background with white accents for a professional appearance, aligned with the Chamber of Commerce's visual identity.
